HackATL 2020 Ambassador

Regardless of where you're attending, we want YOU to represent us as a HackATL Ambassador on your college campus!

With the growing interest of HackATL from campuses all over the states, we have come to realize that we need YOUR help and YOUR experience to reach this broadening audience.

As a HackATL Ambassador, your goals will be 1) to promote HackATL 2020 to your respective communities and 2) be an advocate for HackATL, the largest business hackathon in the Southeast.

What You Will Gain:

  • Marketing experience that strengthens your ability to target groups and communicate effectively to those channels

  • Be part of a team of incredibly driven and passionate students from your class or school

  • Step into the entrepreneurial community in Atlanta, one of the largest booming startup scenes

  • If you are an Emory student interested in joining EEVM, you will get bonus points based on your performance when you apply next semester!

Tasks:

  • Flyer around campus (residence halls, dining halls, other major locations)

  • Post on various social media pages and groups

  • Answer questions about HackATL, or connect students with questions to the HackATL Exec team

  • Be active in the group chat

Our Ideal Candidate Has:

  • A strong work ethic

  • A positive and enthusiastic attitude and is a team player

  • An interest/passion for entrepreneurship

  • Clear communication, presenting, and speaking skills

  • The drive to go above and beyond expectations

  • The ability to commit 1-2 hours per week
